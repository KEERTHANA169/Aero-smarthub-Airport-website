APP BASED RIDESHARES

Explore information about rideshare options for drivers and passengers.

When using rideshares transportation services, there are specific designated areas for pick-up. Determine where your pick-up area is based on your Terminal.

Other taxi companies with an airport permit or agreement, including Uber, Lyft and Wingz, may drop off passengers at any time and pick up passengers with an advance reservation.
